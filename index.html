How Cashiqz Works

From financial data to better daily decisions.

Cashiqz helps teams move from scattered spreadsheets to a simple finance operating rhythm.

01

Connect finance signals

Cashiqz brings together cash balances, open invoices, expenses, budgets, payroll timing, vendor payments, and expected collections so finance decisions start from one reliable view.

02

Forecast future cash

The AI projects likely cash movement across the next weeks and months, then compares expected, cautious, and optimistic scenarios so teams can see where pressure may appear.

Recommend actions

Cashiqz turns the forecast into practical actions such as following up on specific invoices, delaying non-critical purchases, adjusting vendor timing, or protecting payroll cash.
